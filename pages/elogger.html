ELogger

Elogger is the companion app to Elog, the webbox data logger from Enerdis. It lets you access an Elog through the internet, list the connected devices and check their real time values.

Elogger Lite will only list list the first five devices connected to the Elog. For the full list, please purchase the standard version of Elogger.

Elogger is the ideal tool for the commissioning of an energy monitoring system: go from one meter to another, check on your iPhone that they are properly connected to the system, verify their network addresses and that their measures are remote read properly. Elogger is multilingual : English, French, German and Spanish.

Elog webbox data logger is a smart concentrator offering a breakthrough simplification of energy management systems thanks to its universal remote reading and logging: multi brand, multi-format, multi-energy, multi-measures.
